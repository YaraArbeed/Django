My Book List

The Great Gatsby
by F. Scott Fitzgerald
A novel set in the Roaring Twenties that tells the story of the mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and his obsession with the beautiful Daisy Buchanan.
To Kill a Mockingbird
by Harper Lee
A powerful exploration of race and morality in the American South, seen through the eyes of young Scout Finch and her father, Atticus Finch.
1984
by George Orwell
A dystopian novel about a totalitarian regime that uses surveillance, censorship, and fear to control the population, led by the mysterious Big Brother.
